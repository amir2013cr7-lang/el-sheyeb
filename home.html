Our Story

We don't just grill chicken — we keep a fire lit for the neighborhood.

El-Shayeb started as a single charcoal grill in Ras El Nabeh, Beirut in 1987, run by a quiet old man (the "Shayeb" himself) who refused to use gas. Three generations later, the grill is bigger — but the rule is the same.

No shortcuts. No microwaves. No apologies for the smoke.
